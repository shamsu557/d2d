About the Network
Mobilizing Women Across Kano State

The Door-to-Door Women Political Network is a civic initiative ensuring every woman in Kano State is registered and ready to participate fully in the democratic process.

Field registration officers are deployed across all 44 LGAs to collect voter information, track registration status, and monitor progress in real time through our digital platform.

Administrators can generate reports, monitor center-level activity, and ensure no eligible voter is left behind.
